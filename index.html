About Metro Relocators

New York based. Built for the long haul.

Metro Relocators was founded in 2011 by three former long-haul drivers who were tired of watching broker outfits mishandle customers' belongings. We operate our own fleet out of Long Island City, employ our crews directly, and specialize in one thing: getting New Yorkers to their next chapter with nothing broken and nothing hidden in the bill.

Today we're one of the top-rated interstate movers on the East Coast, trusted by families, remote workers, and corporate relocations in all 50 states.
