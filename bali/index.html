FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between KITAS and KITAP? +
KITAS is a temporary stay permit (1–2 years, renewable). KITAP is a permanent stay permit available after holding KITAS for 5 consecutive years. We help you get the right one for your situation and timeline.
How long does PT PMA company setup take? +
Typically 4–8 weeks depending on the business sector and completeness of documents. We handle all government submissions and follow up on your behalf.
Can foreigners own property in Indonesia? +
Foreigners cannot hold freehold (Hak Milik) title directly. However, there are legal structures — Hak Pakai, leasehold, nominee arrangements via PT PMA — that our property team can advise on for your specific situation.
Do I need to visit your office in person? +
For most services, no. Start remotely via WhatsApp, upload documents in your personal cabinet online. Some immigration procedures require in-person attendance — we'll tell you exactly when and where.
How do I track my application status? +
Through your personal cabinet at maxxsgroup.com/cabinet. Real-time status updates, pending document requests, and direct messaging with your dedicated manager — all in one place.
